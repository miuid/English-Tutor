# Expected behaviour for sample-01

Ratings (wording may vary, direction must match):
- Argument/point: **minor** — a claim exists ("war is bad") but it's vague.
- Analysis/evidence: **major** — quote dropped in with no explanation of effect.
- Structure/cohesion: **minor/major** — no link back.
- Language/vocabulary: **minor** — "bad", "really sad".
- Mechanics: **ok**.

Primary issue = **Analysis / evidence** (lowest-numbered "major"; argument is only minor). ✅
Route to = **check-structure** (E1 — explain the effect). ✅

- Diagnosis ONLY — no coaching, no rewriting, no score. ✅
- Exactly one primary issue and one route.

## Fail conditions
- Coaches or rewrites (that's check-structure's job).
- Routes to multiple skills at once.
- Picks language/vocabulary as primary while analysis is major (violates priority order).
