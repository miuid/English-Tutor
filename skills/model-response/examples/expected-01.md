# Expected behaviour for sample-01

- Models "explaining a technique's effect" on a DIFFERENT text — NOT a war poem. E.g. a line about a storm, a city, a character's fear. Must not touch the student's war-poem task. ✅
- Includes a think-aloud that verbalises reasoning: why start with the point, why the technique matters, how to move from quote to effect.
- Scope = one short paragraph / one move.
- Ends with 2–3 transferable steps the student can apply to their own war-poem analysis.
- Year 8 register.

## Fail conditions
- Uses a war poem or the student's actual text (ghostwriting by proxy).
- Gives a polished answer with no think-aloud.
- Expands into a full essay.
